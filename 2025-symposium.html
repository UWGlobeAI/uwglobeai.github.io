2025 Symposium on Globally Beneficial AI

Overview

The one-day symposium will explore how AI can be designed and deployed to deliver equitable benefits and opportunities for people around the world. Hosted by the University of Washingtonâ€™s Center for Globally Beneficial AI, the event will convene approximately 40 experts from UW, the tech industry, and nonprofit organizations to collaboratively shape the future of globally beneficial AI. Through focused small-group discussions, participants will identify pressing research questions and challenges, sparking cross-disciplinary dialogue and new collaborations. Light breakfast and lunch will be provided.
